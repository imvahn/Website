Music Time

Music Time is a music based platformer where the player toggles between different instruments to change the level design and mechanics.

This was a group project completed in three weeks. I created all the music from screatch. I also created the player and level transition animations, level design and mechanics, juice (parallax, decorations, etc).

Chess Data Visualizations

These are five chess visualizations comparing various factors such as move count, board position, and ELO between my chess games and chess games played by Grandmaster Hikaru Nakamura on the chess.com site from February to April 2023. To run these visualizations you must have RStudio installed.
